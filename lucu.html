Introduction to Automotive

Automotive refers to anything related to motor vehicles. It encompasses various aspects such as design, development, manufacturing, marketing, and maintenance of automobiles. The automotive industry plays a crucial role in our modern society, providing transportation and mobility to people across the globe.

With advancements in technology, the automotive industry has witnessed significant innovations over the years. From conventional gasoline-powered vehicles to electric cars, autonomous driving features, and smart connectivity, the automotive landscape continues to evolve, shaping the future of transportation.
